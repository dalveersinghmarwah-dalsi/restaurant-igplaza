# IG Plaza Website Design Brainstorm

## Selected Design Approach: Modern Luxury Minimalism

**Design Movement:** Contemporary luxury branding with minimalist principles and high-end event rental aesthetics.

**Core Principles:**
1. **Obsidian Dominance**: Deep black backgrounds create a gallery-like environment where venue photos and inventory items become the stars
2. **Strategic Rose Gold Accents**: Metallic touches appear only where they matter—CTAs, icons, section dividers, and brand elements
3. **Whitespace as Luxury**: Generous spacing and breathing room convey premium positioning
4. **Typography Hierarchy**: Bold, elegant display fonts paired with clean, readable body text

**Color Philosophy:**
- **Obsidian Black (#0B0B0B)**: Authority, sophistication, and a neutral canvas for showcasing colorful event setups
- **Rose Gold (#E0BFB8)**: Warmth, luxury, and modern elegance—reflects the logo and creates visual interest
- **Champagne Cream (#F7E7CE)**: Softens the contrast and provides breathing room for secondary content
- **Slate Charcoal (#333333)**: Professional hover states and secondary interactions

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric hero with staggered image gallery
- Full-width sections with alternating content/image placement
- Inventory displayed in a masonry-inspired grid with hover effects
- Sticky navigation with rose gold accents
- Diagonal dividers between sections for visual interest

**Signature Elements:**
1. **Rose Gold Divider Lines**: Thin, elegant separators between sections
2. **Hover Image Zoom**: Subtle scale and blur effects on inventory items
3. **Floating CTAs**: Rose Gold buttons with smooth transitions and shadow depth

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth scroll-triggered animations
- Hover states that reveal details without overwhelming
- Touch-friendly mobile interactions
- Loading states with elegant spinners

**Animation Guidelines:**
- Fade-in on scroll for images and text blocks
- Subtle scale transforms (1.02x) on hover for interactive elements
- Smooth color transitions on button states
- Parallax effects on hero section for depth

**Typography System:**
- **Display Font**: Playfair Display or similar elegant serif for headings (h1, h2)
- **Body Font**: Poppins or Lato for readable, modern body text
- **Accent Font**: Montserrat for section labels and CTAs
- **Font Weights**: Bold (700) for headings, Medium (500) for CTAs, Regular (400) for body

---

## Rationale

This design approach positions IG Plaza as a **boutique, high-end event rental service** rather than a generic vendor. The Obsidian Black background makes colorful event setups pop, while the Rose Gold accents create a cohesive brand identity that ties directly to your logo. The minimalist layout emphasizes quality over quantity, allowing each venue photo and inventory item to shine.

The asymmetric layout and strategic whitespace avoid the "corporate" feel of centered, grid-based designs, instead creating a gallery-like experience that feels curated and premium.
